Subway Surfers

What is the Subway Surfers game?

Subway Surfers is an awesome endless running game where you play as Jake and surf through subways as you escape the pursuit of an inspector and his dog after being caught spraying graffiti. Avoid bumping into obstacles such as moving trains, walls, barriers and poles whilst aiming to go as far as possible. Collect coins to unlock gear, power-ups, other characters and boards.

How to Play

  • Arrow keys to move
  • Up to jump
  • Down to roll
  • Spacebar to activate the hoverboard
